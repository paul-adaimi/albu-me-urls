# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

Welcome to **Albu-me** ("we", "our", or "us").  
Albu-me is a mobile application that allows users to create, customize, and order printed photo books.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Albu-me, we may collect the following information:

### Account Information
- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Login credentials  

### Order & Delivery Information
- Shipping address  
- Order details  
- Book customization data  

### Uploaded Content
- Photos uploaded to create photo books  
- Text entered into book pages  

### Device & Technical Information
- Device type  
- Operating system  
- App usage data (for performance and improvements)  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account  
- Process and fulfill your orders  
- Deliver printed photo books  
- Improve app functionality and user experience  
- Provide customer support  
- Comply with legal obligations  

We do **not sell your personal information**.

---

## 3. Camera & Media Access

Albu-me may request access to:

- Camera (to capture photos)  
- Photo library/media storage (to upload images)  

This access is used **only for creating photo books inside the app**.

We do not access your media without your permission.

---

## 4. Payments

Payments are processed through third-party payment providers.  
We do not store your full payment card details on our servers.

Payment providers may collect and process payment information according to their own privacy policies.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using trusted cloud infrastructure.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

- Provide our services

You may request deletion of your account at any time.
