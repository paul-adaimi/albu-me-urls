# Albu-me

Create beautiful personalized photo books directly from your phone.

Albu-me makes it easy to turn your favorite memories into a custom photo book you can design, preview, and order in just a few steps.

---

## Features

### Create your photo book
Choose your photos and build a personalized book directly in the app.

### Customize every detail
Adjust layouts, pages, and design elements to make your book your own.

### Preview before ordering
See how your photo book looks before placing your order.

### Easy ordering
Complete your order directly from the app with a simple and smooth experience.

---

## How It Works

1. Upload your photos
2. Design your book
3. Preview your creation
4. Place your order

---

## Why Albu-me

Albu-me is designed to make photo book creation simple, modern, and enjoyable. Whether you want to preserve a special trip, a family event, or everyday memories, Albu-me helps you turn moments into something you can keep forever.
